# Premier League Statistics

This folder contains Premier League Transfer Data scraped from FBREF.

Header | Definition
---|---------
`Player` | Name of the player
`Nation` | Nationality of the player 
`Pos` | Position(s) most commonly played by the player
`Squad` | Player's team
`Age` | Age of the player
`Born` | Year player was born
`MP` | Matches played by the player
`Starts` | Games started by the player
`Min` | Minutes played by the player
`90s` | Minutes played divided by 90
`Gls` | Goals scored or allowed
`Ast` | Assists
`G+A` | Goals and Assists
`G-PK` | Non-Penalty Goals
`PK` | Penalty kicks made
`PKatt` | Penalty kicks attempted
`CrdY` | Yellow cards earned
`CrdR` | Red cards earned
`xG` | Expected Goals
`npxG` | Non-Penalty expected Goals
`xAG` | Expected Assisted Goals
`npxG+xAG` | Non-Penalty Expected Goals plus Assisted Goals
`Season` | Season of the transfer

Source: [FBREF](https://fbref.com/en/comps/9/Premier-League-Stats)
