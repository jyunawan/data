# LaLiga Transfer Data

This folder contains LaLiga Transfer Data scraped from TransferMarkt.
(Currently, the 2025-2026 season transfer data only contains data from the summer transfer window)

Header | Definition
---|---------
`player_name` | Name of the player
`player_age` | Age of the player
`player_nationality` | Nationality/Nationalities of the player 
`player_position` | Position the player plays in
`market_value` | Market value of the player
`from_club` | Club the player transferred from
`to_club` | Club the player transferred to
`transfer_fee` | Reported transfer fee of the player
`transfer_season` | Season of the transfer

Source: [TransferMarkt](https://www.transfermarkt.us/laliga/transfers/wettbewerb/ES1)
