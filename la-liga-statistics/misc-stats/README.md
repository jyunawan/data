# La Liga Miscellaneous Statistics

This folder contains La Liga Miscellaneous Statistics scraped from FBREF.

Header | Definition
---|---------
`Rk` | Count of the rows
`Player` | Name of the player
`Nation` | Nationality of the player
`Pos` | Position most commonly played by the player
`Squad` | Team player plays for
`Age` | Age of the player
`Born` | Year the player was born
`90s` | Minutes played divided by 90
`CrdY` | Yellow cards
`CrdR` | Red cards
`2CrdY` | Second yellow card
`Fls` | Fouls commited
`Fld` | Fouls drawn
`Off` | Offsides
`Crs` | Crosses
`Int` | Interceptions
`TklW` | Tackles won
`PKwon` | Penalty kicks won
`PKcon` | Penalty kicks conceded
`OG` | Own goals
`Recov` | Number of loose balls recovered
`aerials_Won` | Aerials won
`aerials_Lost` | Aerials lost
`aerials_Won%` | Percentage of aerials won
`Season` | Season the statistic was taken from

Source: [FBREF](https://fbref.com/en/comps/9/Premier-League-Stats)
